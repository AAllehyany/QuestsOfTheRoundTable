1.Player is asked if he wants to sponsor the quest.
2.Once a player decides to sponsor the quest, he has the choice of playing cards to the field to set up the quest.
3.Players are asked if they want to join the quest, if they decide to join the quest, he is able to play cards to the field to defeat
the quest that the sponsor set up.
4.Once the quest is over, players get cards from the deck.
5.Players are asked if they want to join the tournement. If they decide to join the tournement,they will each get a card from the deck and
they are able to play cards to the field to battle with each others.
6.Events happen in between some of the tournement and quest, where players have a chance to discard cards, draw cards, earn extra shields 
after the events take place.
